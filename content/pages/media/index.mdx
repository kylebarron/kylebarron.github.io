## Videos

### Big geospatial visualization with Lonboard and Kyle Barron

May 1, 2024, [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAjabS0GTdo)

A conversation with Matt Forrest about [Lonboard](https://github.com/developmentseed/lonboard), a new Python package that solves the problem of visualizing massive geospatial datasets in Python. Learn about the motivations behind the project, some of the tech under the hood, and of course a demo!
